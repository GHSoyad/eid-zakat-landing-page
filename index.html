What is Zakat in Islam?

Zakat is the third pillar of Islam. It signifies the wealth a Muslim pays to the poor and less privileged people. Zakat is a compulsory act of worship and represents the responsibility and duty the richer have towards those in need. The word Zakat means both 'purification' and 'growth'.
